About the project

What is TonForce?

TonForce is an innovative decentralized platform on the TON (The Open Network) blockchain, built by the Telegram team. The project implements a 3×8 matrix system for distributing funds via a transparent smart contract, giving participants the opportunity to earn TON cryptocurrency without intermediaries, administrators, or centralized servers.

🌟 Why TonForce

Unlike traditional MLM projects, where an administrator controls payouts and can freeze funds at any time, TonForce runs on an immutable smart contract on the TON network. This means that:

  • Funds cannot be stolen — they flow directly from participant to participant via the blockchain
  • No one can reverse a transaction — all operations are final and publicly verifiable
  • No hidden fees — 100% of the payment goes to your referrer, aside from a small network gas fee (~0.2 TON)
  • Full anonymity — only a crypto wallet is required, no documents
  • 24/7 availability — the smart contract runs around the clock
  • Hardcoded prices — level prices are fixed in the code and cannot be changed by the owner

💎 How the 3×8 matrix works

The TonForce matrix structure consists of 3 direct referrals across 8 levels. Each level has its own activation price, which triples with each step: from 5 TON at the first level to 10,935 TON at the eighth. Activating a level entitles you to receive payouts from referrals for 365 days.

🔗 4-level deep referral program

When you buy a level, the payout goes to your referrer at a specific depth:

  • Levels 1 and 5 → payout goes to your direct referrer (R1)
  • Levels 2 and 6 → payout goes to your referrer's referrer (R2)
  • Levels 3 and 7 → payout goes to the 3rd depth level (R3)
  • Levels 4 and 8 → payout goes to the 4th depth level (R4)

If the upline at the relevant level is missing or inactive, the payout is routed to the project wallet. This structure lets you earn passive income not only from direct referrals, but from a whole team 4 levels deep.

📱 Launches via Telegram

For convenience, TonForce runs as a Telegram Mini App inside the bot @tonforce_bot. No need to download extra apps — open the bot, connect a TON wallet (Tonkeeper, MyTonWallet, Telegram Wallet), and start earning.

🔒 Verifiable security

The TonForce v4 smart contract address (Tact 1.6.13, 537 lines) can be verified in public TON blockchain explorers: tonscan.org and tonviewer.com. All transactions, balances, and the participant list are visible 24/7. The source code is published on GitHub.

Questions and answers

Frequently asked

Answers to the key questions about the TonForce platform.

What is TonForce?
TonForce is a decentralized matrix platform on the TON blockchain. The smart contract implements a 3×8 matrix with a referral program 4 levels deep. All payouts go directly between participants without intermediaries. The contract has passed an internal technical review.
How do I start earning?
1) Install a wallet (Tonkeeper or Telegram Wallet). 2) Buy at least 6 TON on an exchange (Binance, Bybit, OKX) or via Wallet. 3) Open @tonforce_bot. 4) Connect your wallet and activate the first level for 5.2 TON (5 TON + 0.2 for gas and payouts).
Is the smart contract secure?
The contract has passed an internal technical review. Protections against reentrancy, integer overflow, unauthorized withdrawal, and DoS are verified. All 537 lines of Tact 1.6.13 code have been reviewed. Contract address: tonscan.org. This is an internal review, not a third-party audit — we recommend personally reviewing the code on GitHub.
Can the owner steal my funds?
No. According to the review, the project owner has no administrative functions to change subscriptions, the tree, or user balances. Prices are hardcoded and cannot be changed after deployment. The contract does not hold user funds — all TON is distributed in the same transaction.
How much can I earn?
It depends on activity. With 3 referrals on 3 levels you receive about 195 TON per month. With 10 referrals and all 8 levels — potentially more than 150,000 TON. Use the calculator above for your own scenario.
What if I don't invite anyone?
If your referrer doesn't have the relevant level active, the payout from your purchase goes to the project wallet. If you don't invite referrals yourself — thanks to the overflow system, new registrations in the matrix can land under you automatically. Earnings without referrals are not guaranteed.
Where can I see statistics?
The Mini App has "Referral tree", "Top", and "History" tabs. All transactions are also visible in public explorers: tonscan.org and tonviewer.com. The contract provides 13 get-methods for free state reads.
What are the fees?
Only TON network gas — ~0.2 TON above the level price. This buffer covers gas for tree payouts and leaves ~0.05 TON in the contract for storage fee. The platform takes no fees of its own — payouts go directly to the referrer.
How long does a level last?
Each activated level lasts 365 days (expiration timestamp stored as uint64). After that you need to renew it by paying the same amount. Buying a new level (say, the second) does not cancel previous ones.
Which blockchain does it run on?
TonForce runs on TON Mainnet — the blockchain developed by the Telegram team. TON stands out for high speed (millions of transactions per second) and low fees. The contract is written in Tact 1.6.13.
What are the risks?
Technical risks are minimized by the internal review. But participation in a matrix MLM program carries economic risk: your profit depends on attracting referrals. If you don't attract enough referrals, you may not recover your investment. Only invest funds whose loss won't affect your financial well-being.
